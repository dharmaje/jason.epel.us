    Jason Epel

    VP of Engineering & Technology at eSUB · SaaS, Cloud, Data & AI · Scaling high-performing engineering organizations

    I lead engineering and technology organizations that ship durable software and run the systems a modern company depends on. As VP of Engineering & Technology at eSUB, I own the software, cloud, data, and IT functions behind a construction SaaS platform used by subcontractors across the United States and Canada.

    Over nearly seven years at eSUB I have grown from Senior Manager to Director to VP, expanding scope from a single product team to the full engineering organization and the business systems that support sales, finance, security, and operations. I set the technical strategy and drove the execution behind our modernization on AWS, where my teams delivered a stack built on React, React Native, serverless services, and streaming data, along with the analytics and BI foundation that turns product data into decisions.

    My background spans SaaS, mobile, public and private APIs, and cloud infrastructure across AWS, Azure, and GCP, across construction, medical and biotech, financial services, higher education, legal, government, and ecommerce. I am also an Agile practitioner and coach who has introduced and scaled Scrum and Kanban across multiple companies, moving teams from waterfall to predictable, quality-first delivery.

    Most recently I have led our adoption of AI, working hands-on with Claude Code alongside my team to build internal AI services, developer tooling, and automation that change how we deliver. I care about clear priorities, healthy teams, and technology that earns its keep.
